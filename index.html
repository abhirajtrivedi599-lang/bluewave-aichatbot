Health Awareness

Important preventive guidance that people should know

Hand Hygiene

Washing hands with soap for 20 seconds helps reduce transmission of many common infections.

Vaccination

Keeping vaccinations updated helps prevent serious infectious diseases and protects the community.

Heat Safety

Hydration, avoiding peak heat, and wearing light clothing reduce the risk of dehydration and heatstroke.

Mental Health

Persistent low mood, anxiety, sleep problems, or hopelessness deserve attention and professional support.

Stroke FAST Signs

Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty, and time to act quickly are life-saving warning signs.

Heart Health

Regular walking, lower salt intake, smoking avoidance, and blood pressure checks reduce long-term risk.
